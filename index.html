2017 Organ Donations

Visualization 2017 organ donations in the United States built by Paul Cunningham April 2017, link to GitHub repository below.
Data pulled from U.S. Department of Health & Human Services Organ Procurement and Transplant Network and
from a Milliman Research Report, "2017 U.S. Organ and Tissue Transplant Cost Estimates and Discussion".
Full references available in read me file on this project's GitHub Page.

Area of squares is relative to total number of donations made of each organ in 2017.
Hover over each and click for more details.
